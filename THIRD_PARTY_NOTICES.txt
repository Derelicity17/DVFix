This project is code-only and does not ship third-party binaries.

DVFix can invoke external tools that you install separately. Those tools are
licensed by their respective authors. Check their licenses before
redistributing any binaries.

- FFmpeg (LGPL/GPL depending on build) - https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html
- dovi_tool (MIT) - https://github.com/quietvoid/dovi_tool
- libplacebo (LGPL-2.1+) - https://github.com/haasn/libplacebo
